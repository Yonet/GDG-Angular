Why Angular?

SPA, data binding, MVC, routing, history, testing, jqLite, templates, factories for code sharing, dynamicly injecting new features through dependency injection, directives, ViewModel, Validation... It is fully extensible and works well with other libraries. Every feature can be modified or replaced to suit your unique development workflow and feature needs
